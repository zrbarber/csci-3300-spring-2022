I haven't visited many places outside of the state, however, the places I have visited were quite great. It's only the 2 places:

  • Atlanta, Georgia
  • New Orleans, Louisiana (My Personal Favorite)

Atlanta was very enjoyable because I spent time at the Aquarium, CNN Headquarters, and Six Flags. All were really cool to see.

New Orleans, on the other hand, was very cool for other reasons. Being able to explore the cultures, foods, and languages of New Orleans was such a treat. The jazz, biscuit beignets, and various shops were very different from here in Clarksville.
