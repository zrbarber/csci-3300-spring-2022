Major events in Computer Science

  1. The Electronic Numerical Integrator and Computer (ENIAC) was created in 1946.
  2. ARPANET was established in 1967 creating the first computer network.
  3. The World Wide Web (WWW) was invented by Tim Berners-Lee in 1989.

My thoughts

These events were important leaps in technology that are still utilized today.
I find the computer science field to be so fascinating because of the possibility of leaps like these.
Being able to discover or witness a leap in my lifetime will forever be a long-term goal of mine.
